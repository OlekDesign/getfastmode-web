# FastMode — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Health-conscious adults 25–50 who are tired of calorie counting and want a sustainable lifestyle habit. They use the app daily — in the morning before breakfast, checking their fast progress on the commute. They are curious about the science, not obsessed with the grind. They want to feel informed and in control, not pressured.

### Brand Personality
**Motivating, energetic, expressive** — but grounded in calm confidence, not aggression. Think: a knowledgeable friend who gets genuinely excited about the science and wants to share it with you. Not a coach yelling at you. Not a meditation guru whispering. Someone in between — warm, direct, a little bit enthusiastic.

Three words: **purposeful · warm · confident**

### Reference & Anti-Reference
- **Reference**: Headspace / Calm — smooth rhythm, organic feel, breathing space, things that feel alive without shouting
- **Anti-reference (hard)**: Fitness/gym aggro (dark bg, neons, beast mode energy), corporate SaaS (gradient hero, 3-col feature grid, testimonial avatars), minimalism-for-minimalism (so empty it has no personality)
- **Anti-reference (soft)**: Generic health app (stock salad photos, pastel blob shapes, wellness vibes)

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode. Background: `#FAFAFA`. Warm, slightly tinted neutrals toward brand orange. Typography-led — big confident headings (Bricolage Grotesque ExtraBold) with a precise body font (Inter). The ring timer concept (circle, phases, glow) should inform decorative motifs where appropriate. Sections feel spacious but not empty. Accent colors used purposefully: orange (#F5A623) for energy/primary actions, green (#4CAF7D) for the Fat Burn phase milestone moments.

### Design Principles
1. **Science as warmth** — data and facts are presented as reassuring, not clinical. Numbers feel like milestones, not metrics.
2. **Earn every pixel** — no decorative cards, no icon-above-heading grids, no filler sections. Every element earns its space.
3. **Momentum over perfection** — the interface conveys progress and forward motion. Streaks, phases, timers — always moving.
4. **Confident whitespace** — generous spacing signals quality. But it's never empty for empty's sake. Every section has a clear job.
5. **Orange is scarce** — the primary accent works because it's rare. Use it for one thing per section: a number, a CTA, a milestone label.

### Color System
- Background: `oklch(98% 0.005 60)` ≈ `#FAFAFA` warm-tinted
- Surface: `oklch(100% 0 0)` = white
- Text primary: `oklch(8% 0.01 60)` ≈ `#0A0A0A`
- Text secondary: `oklch(40% 0.01 60)` ≈ `#606060`
- Primary (orange): `oklch(72% 0.16 55)` ≈ `#F5A623`
- Success (fat burn green): `oklch(65% 0.14 155)` ≈ `#4CAF7D`
- Accent (deep orange): `oklch(65% 0.18 40)` ≈ `#FF6B35`

### Typography
- Heading: **Bricolage Grotesque ExtraBold** — expressive, warm, confident
- Body: **Inter** — established in project, precise and readable
- Scale: fluid `clamp()` for headings, fixed `rem` for UI labels
